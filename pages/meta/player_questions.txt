====== Player Questions ======

The following is a list of questions to help players flesh out their character's personality and backstory.

===== Wants and Desires =====
  * You come across a million gold pieces. What do you buy first?
  * What is your most prized possession?


===== Relationships =====
  * Who is your biggest role model?
  * Who do you want to connect with, but will never see again?

===== Values =====
  * How does one overcome fear?
