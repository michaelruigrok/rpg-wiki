====== NPC Creation ======
  * For most NPCs, simpler is better.
  * Name, sex, race, class.
  * Extra performance traits: 
     * high/med/low voice, manner of speaking (strict, loose), germanic/latin/french word choice

  * Ideals, desires, fears
  * Disposition to players
  * Skills, equipment, mannerisms


  * Use NPCs as a viewpoint into the world. Give them opinions about the current events, other characters, and their way of life.
  * Show the players how they are perceived within the world
